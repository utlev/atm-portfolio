# Capstone — A Week in the Life of Dutch Airspace

> Planned for **Weeks 9–10** of the learning plan.

## Goal

A single coherent analysis of one week of LVNL / Maastricht UAC traffic, combining the
themes of all six mini-projects: volume, sectorisation, separation, fuel, ATFM delay,
and environmental performance. Delivered as a narrated Jupyter notebook suitable for
showing in an interview.

## Structure (planned)

1. The week in numbers — volume and peak hours
2. Where the traffic is — FIR / sector load
3. How safely it ran — separation, TCAS RA events
4. How efficiently — CDO/CCO compliance, fuel burn estimates
5. How punctually — ATFM delay breakdown
6. What this tells us — narrative conclusion
